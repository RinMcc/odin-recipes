Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for homemade vegetarian lasagna.

It should take approximately 3 hours and serves 6.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Wine
  • Tomato Paste
  • Vegetable Stock
  • Bay Leaf
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  3. In a large saucepan, cook and stir onions, garlic, carrots, and celery in olive oil. Stir in tomatoes, red wine, tomato paste, and vegetable stock. Season with bay leaf, thyme, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium low heat. Whisk in flour and cook stirring constantly for 2 minutes. Whisk in milk, a little at a time, increase heat to medium-high, and cook, stirring constantly, until sauce is thick and bubbly. Stir in nutmeg, and remove from heat.
  5. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce in the bottom of a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with 1/3 of the lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the remaining tomato sauce, 1/2 the spinach, and 1/2 the mozzarella cheese. Top with another 1/3 of the lasagna noodles, and 1/2 the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat layers, and top with remaining noodles and sauce. Sprinkle top with grated Parmesan cheese and 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake lasagna in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Let lasagna stand 10 minutes before serving.
